Ram Charan

About Ram Charan

The heartthrob of Telugu cinema began his acting career in 2007 with super hit movie "Chirutha" starring alongside Delhi-based model Neha Sharma. Ram Charan won accolades for his performance in the movie. He received the CineMAA award for Best Male Debut, Filmfare for Best Actor and the prestigious Nandi special jury award for "Chirutha".

Following the success of his film, the actor starred in numerous movies that established him as a youth icon. Director SS Rajamouli's historical drama film "Magadheera" which was released in 2009 showed him in a new avatar. The actor portrayed a double role in the movie that once again won him the Nandi special jury award and the Filmfare award for Telugu Best actor.

"Magadheera" went on to become a super successful film and was reportedly the highest grosser in the history of Telugu cinema. Following the success of his films, Ram Charan picked up scripts that had all commercial elements. The actor is continuing his winning streak at the box office with the recently released "Naayak" in which he once again starred with Kajal Agarwal, his "Magadheera" heroine.
